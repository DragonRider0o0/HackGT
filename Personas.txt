Personas

The primary persona is Dan.

3 types of personas: 

	(1) Victims

		Maria is a 25 year old graduate student.
		She is an earthquake victim, and wants to let her family know she's ok.
		However, she has limited mobility because she is injured and stuck.

		Dan is a father who has a wife and two kids, ages 2 and 5.
		He wants to get his family to a safe place during an earthquake in Southern California.


	(2) Responders

		James is with Red Cross Disaster Relief and needs to coordinate survivors and allocate
		resources for rescue efforts

	(3) Red Cross Adminimstration

		Governor Brown needs a system to quickly notify victims and responders and quickly 
		coordinate relief efforts.
